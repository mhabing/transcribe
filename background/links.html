Nationaal archief

this is the digitalized archive of all the VOC documents. There are 4 or 5 archives in the Netherlands, Amsterdam, Hoorn, etc, that contain copies of letters, missives and orders that were either sent or received from the colonies. there is an index in xml format which can be easily searched. But from title it is not always clear what the true content is. the documents are high-res jpg's of the individual pages. these could automatically downloaded by scripting. so these could be left on the server.
